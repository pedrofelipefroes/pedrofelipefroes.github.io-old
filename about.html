Nice to meet you!
I am Froes.

Also called Pedro, PF, or that nerdy design guy. I am a Designer and Developer graduated in Computer Engineering and currently working at Avenue Code.

Creativity has always been a passion of mine, and I'm excited to apply it in both designing and coding. In addition, I highly value being organized, and this helps me in documenting my designs and developing practical code.

Being creative and organized has always been natural to me, but new skills came along the way as I grew, studied, and matured as a professional. Check out my journey into becoming a Designer and Developer next. And download my resumé here.

Drawing is a passion that sprung at an early age. I’ve explored a lot of styles over the years and even sold some to pay for my pre-college prep course.

Started majoring in Civil Engineering, but that wasn’t working out for me. Went on to an academic exchange in Chicago and unleashed the developer inside as I started to learn to code.

Enrolled in Computer Engineering at CEFET-MG and leaned even more about software development. The course was ranked the 2nd best Computer Engineering graduation in Brazil in 2018.

Got into a scholarship on UI/UX where I could learn and explore more how to bring my artsy side to computing. Found myself as a designer and developer.

Started working at Avenue Code. This amazing company gave me room to explore Visual Design, UI and UX Design, and Front-end Development.

Matured inside the Avenue Code, and got to work with Email Design and designing for Marketing. Even launched my own Design System project inside the company!

I believe there's always room for growing and improving, so I'm eager to see what the future holds—and I'd be excited to team up and shape our stories together. Contact me!
